Sightsavers bags the prestigious HCL Grant 2018

At a power packed ceremony at the HCL campus in Delhi-NCR, Sightsavers received the HCL award of 5 Crore INR for the extensive work on Inclusive Education in Rajasthan, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha and Madhya Pradesh.

NGO Pratham raises 4 million USD

One of India's largest non-governmental education organisations, Pratham, has raised more than USD 4 million for providing quality education to underprivileged children in the country.

Kolkata teen sets up NGO to educate street-kids

A student of Modern High School has become a social change-agent by bringing education into the lives of dozens of underprivileged and street kids. Sangam, a NGO started by her, collect books, exercise books, stationary and other educational materials and distribute them among the poor children.
